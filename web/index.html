Methodology

  • 1999–present stats from nflverse via weekly player stats joined to official schedules.
  • 1995–1998 stats supplemented from Pro-Football-Reference season passing tables.
  • Age filters use birth date vs. game date. Legacy 1995–1998 rows use September 1 proxy dates.
  • Only passing stats recorded with Jacksonville (JAX/JAC) count toward franchise records.
